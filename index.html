About The Event

Its National Workshop on AI/ML organized by Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT), Department of Computing, with support from the African Centre for Technology Studies (ACTS). This workshop presents an opportunity for PhD and Masters students to showcase their research work in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. We invite students, faculty members, researchers, and professionals from academia and industry to participate in this event. The theme of the workshop is Supporting Postgraduate Students in Publication. The workshop will provide a platform for postgraduate students to present their research work and receive feedback from experts in the field. The workshop aims to foster collaborations, promote networking and encourage knowledge sharing among participants. The workshop will feature presentations from PhD and masterâ€™s students on their research work in AI/ML. The presentations will be followed by a panel discussion on postgraduate students' challenges in publishing their research work. The panel discussion will be moderated by field experts and will allow participants to ask questions and share their experiences. In addition to the presentations and panel discussions, the workshop will include networking opportunities where participants can meet and interact with other researchers and professionals in the field. Take advantage of this opportunity to engage with peers and experts in the field and learn about the latest trends and developments in AI/ML research trends and developments.
